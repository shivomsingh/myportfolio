How Internet Works

In our daily life all of us come across the intenet things one way or another. Sometimes, we don't pay attention but generally, we have alwyas this curiosity that how Internet Works!! If you also belong to that group who always wanted to know the mechanism of Working of Internet then that's amazing, you are at the right place to know all aboout it.
Let us understand by simple example, if i ask you to open drive "C" in your computer what will you do? You will go to "My Computer" then you will open "C" drive folder there. So, content of this "C" folder is visible to me now. Now, consider your computer as a server and whenever you type a website in an url bar, you are putting an address. Your request will reach at your address in the global server and fetch all the information present at that particular address and will show that information on your screen. Here, this communication between your computer and the internet happens by an internet communication protocol i.e, "HTTPS". This communication prtocol provides a secure online experience to the user.
